Self-Reflection Savory Stew

Sip your own brilliance in every self-admiring spoonful and Reflect your inner greatness!

This soup is a reflection of your culinary greatness, with a savory blend of ego, entitlement, and a dash of vanity. A must-try for those who believe they're the fairest of them all.


    Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of self-admiration
  • 2 tablespoons of ego
  • A pinch of entitlement
  • A handful of compliments (preferably self-given)
  • A generous splash of vanity
  • A dash of arrogance
  • A sprinkle of delusion

    Instructions:

  1. Begin by preheating your self-esteem to an all-time high. You should feel superior before even starting the cooking process.
  2. In a pot of boiling self-admiration, add the ego and entitlement. Stir vigorously while reminding yourself how amazing you are.
  3. Gradually incorporate the handful of compliments. Make sure they're all about you and your incredible qualities.
  4. Pour in a generous splash of vanity and a dash of arrogance. Let it simmer, and don't forget to gaze at your reflection in the pot to reaffirm your exceptionalism.
  5. As a finishing touch, sprinkle a little delusion over the soup to enhance the flavor of self-deception.
  6. Serve this delightful creation in a mirror to ensure you can admire yourself while enjoying the Self-Reflection Savory Stew.
